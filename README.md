## PROBLEM STATEMENT <br>
A xyz store wants to create an annual sales report.So that, It can understand their customers and imporve its sales in upcoming years.<br>
## DASHBOARD MUST PROVIDE INSIGHTS FOR THE QUESTIONS<br>
- Which month got the highest sales and orders.
- Who purchased more, men or women.
- What are different order status.
- List top 5 states contributing to the sales.
- Relation between age and gender based on number of orders.
- Which channel is contributing to maximum sales.
## INSIGHTS<br>
- March month got the highest sales and orders which is approx 1.80M.
- women(~69%) purchases more compared to men.
- There are four order status which are, Delivered(~92%), Returned(~3%), Cancelled(~3%), Refunded(~2%).
- Top 5 status contributing to the sales are Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, Tamil Nadu.
- Women of age group(30 - 49 yrs) are contributing maximum to the sales.
- Amazon contributing to maximum sales (~35%).
## CONCLUSION<br>
Target women customers of age group (30-49 yrs) who lives in Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh by showing ads/offers available on amazon, flipkart.
